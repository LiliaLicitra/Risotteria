About Us

Risotteria is a restaurant in the center of Finland. Our goal is to serve real Italian Risotto with a combination of Finnish products in order to create innovative dishes enriched with typical Italian culinary culture. In addition, our restaurant provides a selection of Italian wine that perfectly match the proposed menu. The quality of our products reflects the professionality of our staff and our intent to spread Italian culinary culture in Finland. In 2020 we launched a new concept that will provide customers the possibility to buy on the website a box with all the ingredients necessary to cook the risotto at home..
